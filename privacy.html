Privacy Policy

Effective 1 July 2026 · Interim (v1)

Short version: Your checklist never leaves your browser. We don't have accounts, ads, or trackers. The only thing you can hand us is an email address — and only if you choose to.

Who runs this

ClaimReady is an independent, free educational tool. It is not an insurance company, agent, or broker, and it is not affiliated with any insurer.

What we collect

  • Your checklist progress — stored only on your device. The items you check off are saved in your browser's localStorage. They are never sent to us or anyone else. Clear your browser data, or use the "Reset this list" button, and it's gone.
  • Your email address — only if you opt in. If you type your email into the optional "early access" box and submit it, we collect that email so we can tell you when new features launch. Nothing else is required, and the tool works fully without it.
  • "Email myself this checklist" collects nothing. That button just opens your own email app with the list pre-filled. We never see it — it goes wherever you send it.

What we don't do

  • No accounts, logins, or passwords.
  • No advertising and no ad networks.
  • No analytics or tracking cookies, and no selling or renting of your information — ever.
  • No uploading of your photos, receipts, serial numbers, or any documents. (v1 doesn't handle files at all.)

Third parties

  • Hosting. The site is served by GitHub Pages. Like any web host, GitHub may automatically log standard technical data (such as your IP address and browser type) to operate and secure the service, under GitHub's privacy statement. We don't receive or use that data.
  • Email delivery. If you submit your email for early access, it is stored and processed by a third-party form/newsletter provider solely so we can email you about ClaimReady. We use it for that purpose only.

Your choices

  • Don't want anything stored? Don't enter your email — the whole tool still works.
  • On the list and want off? Every email we send will include an unsubscribe link, or you can contact us to be removed and have your address deleted.
  • Want your local progress gone? Use "Reset this list" or clear your browser storage.

Children

ClaimReady is intended for adults managing their own insurance and is not directed to children under 13. We don't knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

This is an interim policy for an early-stage tool and may change as ClaimReady adds features (for example, a future document vault). We'll update the date above when it does. Material changes to how we handle email will be reflected here before they take effect.

Contact

Questions or requests (including deleting your email from our list): open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
