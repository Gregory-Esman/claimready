Why this exists

The system is backward on purpose

They disclose, they don't teach

The rules live in a policy booklet written by lawyers. Legally "told you." Practically, nobody reads it — until it's too late.

Your agent won't coach you

Agents sell and service policies. Easier, better-documented claims are worse for the insurer's bottom line. Nobody's paid to prep you.

You learn at claim time

The moment you need proof is the moment you discover you don't have it — the working photo, the reliable date, the receipt. Now it's a denial.
